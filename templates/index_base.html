About Us

Humanitarian Awards Global exists to identify, honour and celebrate a cross-section of the nations most influential Non-Profit Organizations from a wide range of industries, who are committed to the growth and community service. We believe that in putting the spotlight on these non-profit organisations it will not only celebrate them but also build a strong platform for them by giving a voice and opportunities whiles building a positive attitude in the world.
