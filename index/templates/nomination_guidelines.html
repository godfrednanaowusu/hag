Nomination Guidelines



Eligibility:

To be eligible for nomination, an individual or company must have worked or volunteered for a charity within the borders of Ghana, Africa, and the world at some time in the year from January to December.


Nominators:

Nominations are invited from voluntary and community sector organizations and members of the public.


Selection Process:

  1. One winner will be selected from each award category, but all nominees will be recognized.
  2. Nomination details, including nomination information, may be used in publicity, including photographs, media, and/or articles for promotional purposes.
  3. The committee reserves the right not to award prizes in any category if they consider nominations to be unsuitable.
  4. If a nomination is for a volunteer who is under 18 years old, their parents or guardian must give their permission.

Submission Requirements:

For each company entry, a write-up of not more than 1,000 words, excluding attachments, profiles of the company, and brochures, media, is required.


Awards:

Winners will receive a plaque and certificate. There is no guaranteed cash or alternative award available in whole or in part. Any financial awards are subject to board decisions and sponsorship for the year.
